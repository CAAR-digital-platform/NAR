Technical Risks

Specialised insurance for construction projects, IT systems and industrial equipment — covering technical risks from planning through to completion.

Contractors' Plant & Machinery Insurance

CPM

Covers mobile and stationary construction equipment against sudden, unforeseen damage occurring on or off the construction site during operation, maintenance or transport.

Covered Risks
Accidental physical damage
Operator error and mishandling
Mechanical and electrical breakdown
Fire and explosion
Theft of machinery
Third-party liability
Optional — essential for construction companies with heavy equipment.

IT All Risks Insurance

Informatique TR

Comprehensive protection for all computer hardware, software and data against accidental damage, theft, power surges and other technical hazards.

Covered Risks
Accidental physical damage
Power surge and electrical damage
Theft of IT equipment
Data loss and reconstruction costs
Fire and water damage
Cost of temporary replacement equipment
Optional — recommended for all businesses dependent on IT infrastructure.

Construction & Erection All Risks

CAR / EAR

Covers civil engineering and erection projects against material damage and third-party liability arising throughout the construction or assembly period.

Covered Risks
Damage to works under construction
Construction materials and supplies
Third-party bodily injury
Third-party property damage
Natural perils (storm, flood, earthquake)
Removal of debris costs
Optional — widely required by project owners and lenders.
