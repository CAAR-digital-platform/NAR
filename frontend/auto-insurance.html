Auto Insurance

Complete vehicle insurance solutions — mandatory civil liability and optional guarantees to protect you, your passengers and third parties on Algerian roads.

Motor Insurance

RC Auto

Mandatory for all vehicles registered in Algeria. Covers civil liability towards third parties in the event of a traffic accident, ensuring full legal compliance.

Covered Risks
Bodily injury to third parties
Material damage to third parties
Passenger liability coverage
Hit-and-run accident coverage
Legal defense and recourse
Uninsured driver protection
Mandatory — required for all vehicles on public roads in Algeria.

Roadside Assistance

Assistance

Available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, throughout the national territory. Fast response to keep you safe wherever you are.

Services Included
Vehicle towing to nearest garage
On-site mechanical assistance
Replacement vehicle provision
Hotel accommodation if stranded
Repatriation of vehicle and passengers
24/7 telephone support line
Optional — highly recommended for all vehicle owners.

Glass Breakage Assistance

Bris de Glace

Covers repair or replacement of all glass elements of your vehicle following impact, vandalism, or adverse weather conditions.

Covered Elements
Windshield damage and cracks
Side and rear windows
Sunroof glass panel
Rear-view mirror glass
Headlight and indicator lenses
Emergency replacement service
Optional — available as an add-on to any auto policy.
